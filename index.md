# Privacy Policy for KwikSaif

**Last Updated: December 7, 2025**

**Your Safety Is In Your Hand**

## Introduction

KwikSaif is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application KwikSaif (the "App").

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

When you create an account and use KwikSaif, we collect the following personal information:

- **Account Information**: Full name, username, email address, phone number, and password
- **Profile Information**: Profile picture (optional)
- **Location Data**: Your location coordinates and location name for incident reporting, SOS alerts, and trip timer features
- **Emergency Contacts**: Names and phone numbers of contacts you designate for emergency alerts

### Incident and Community Data

When you report incidents or interact with the community features, we collect:

- **Incident Reports**: Description, category (Robbery, Kidnap, Accident, Electricity, Crime, Flood, Traffic), location, and any media (photos/videos) you choose to upload
- **Community Interactions**: Comments on incidents, upvotes, downvotes, and reposts
- **Search Queries**: Searches for incidents or users within the app

### Trip Timer Data

When you use the Trip Timer feature, we collect:

- **Trip Information**: Start time, end time, destination, duration, and associated emergency contacts
- **Safety PIN**: A PIN code for trip verification (stored locally on your device)

### Technical Data

We automatically collect certain technical information:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data**: App features used, timestamps of activity
- **Authentication Data**: Login sessions and authentication tokens

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide Safety Services**: Enable SOS alerts, trip timers, and incident reporting
- **Send Emergency Alerts**: Notify your emergency contacts via SMS when you trigger an SOS or when a trip timer expires
- **Display Community Content**: Show incident reports in your area and enable community interactions
- **Improve the App**: Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
- **Communicate with You**: Send important notifications about your account or app updates
- **Ensure Security**: Protect against fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access

## Location Data

KwikSaif requires access to your location for core safety features:

- **Incident Reporting**: To tag the location of reported incidents
- **SOS Alerts**: To share your current location with emergency contacts
- **Trip Timer**: To include your location in safety alerts if a timer expires
- **Community Feed**: To show relevant incidents in your area

You can control location permissions through your device settings. However, disabling location access may limit app functionality.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### Third-Party Service Providers

We use the following third-party services to operate KwikSaif:

- **Firebase (Google)**: For authentication, database storage, and cloud messaging
  - Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- **Cloudinary**: For storing and delivering profile pictures and incident media
  - Privacy Policy: https://cloudinary.com/privacy

### Public Information

The following information may be visible to other KwikSaif users:

- Your username and profile picture
- Incident reports you create (including location and media)
- Comments and interactions on community posts
- Your repost activity

### Emergency Contacts

When you trigger an SOS alert or a trip timer expires, your emergency contacts will receive SMS messages containing:

- Your name
- Your current location (if available)
- The nature of the alert

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage

Your data is stored securely using:

- **Firebase Firestore**: Cloud database for user accounts, incidents, and emergency contacts
- **Cloudinary**: Cloud storage for media files
- **Local Storage**: Certain data (preferences, cached content, safety PINs) is stored locally on your device

### Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures including:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Firebase Authentication for secure access
- Firestore Security Rules to restrict data access
- Secure password hashing

## Data Retention

- **Account Data**: Retained while your account is active
- **Incident Reports**: Retained indefinitely to maintain community safety records
- **Trip Timer Data**: Retained for 30 days after completion
- **Deleted Accounts**: Personal data is deleted within 30 days of account deletion request

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

- **Access**: View your personal information in the app
- **Update**: Edit your profile information at any time
- **Delete**: Request deletion of your account and associated data
- **Emergency Contacts**: Add, modify, or remove emergency contacts
- **Location**: Control location permissions through device settings

To exercise these rights or request account deletion, please contact us at the email provided below.

## Children's Privacy

KwikSaif is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
